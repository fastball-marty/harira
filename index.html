Harira

Ingredients

  • 1 onion (diced)
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 (14oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (14oz) can chickpeas (do not drain)
  • 1 cup dried lentils (rinsed)
  • 3 1/2 cups water

Directions

1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat and add onion. Cook until softened.

2. Add spices and toast for 30 seconds/until fragrant.

3. Stir in tomatoes, chickpeas, lentils, and water. Salt to taste (I use around 2 tsp).

4. Simmer on low, covered, for ~20 minutes, until lentils are soft and cooked.

Notes

Adjust spices to taste. You can also include cumin, coriander, etc.

Throw in carrots, potatoes, garlic, leafy greens, if desired.

Garnish with parsley, cilantro, lemon juice.
